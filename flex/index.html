About

Anzhelika Solovyeva is an assistant professor at the Faculty of Social Sciences, Charles University. In her research, she focuses on the interplay between security and technology, with the primary focus on varying processes of political construction of warfare technologies. Her doctoral dissertation explored the evolution of Russian military thinking with respect to conventional and nuclear weapons since the 19th century. Her publications have appeared or are forthcoming in Oxford University Press, Routledge, Palgrave Macmillan, Rowman and Littlefield, Scopus Q1-ranked and IF journals.
